Tables and their essentials functions 
users – Stores all registered users (residents, staff, admin)

applications – Records of submitted applications

documents – Uploaded files linked to applications

statuses – Tracks the progress/stage of each application

payments – Payment tracking

notifications – System-generated alerts for users

roles – User roles (admin, staff, resident)

qr_codes – Stores generated QR details for verification

logs (optional) – Activity logs for audit purposes
